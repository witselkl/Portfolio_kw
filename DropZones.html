Miltary Drop Zones

Miliary training area of Joint Base Lewis McChord in Tacoma, WA routinely practices miliary excercises that involve dropping cargo from helicopters. This type of training may invlove the dropping of supplies or people. Some times drops involve freight like tanks. This is why drop zones are established to secure the area and minimize the risk of injuries below. Many of the military training lands are not fenced in and occur where the general public might find themselves. Drop zones also provide a way for aviation traffic a way to monitor and control airspace. Due to the restrictions on air space, drop zones are created through a formal process with the FAA.
