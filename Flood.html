100 Year Flood Map

This map stemmed from a land cover project that I helped to design. The final output of the flood modeling was developed by Colorado State University Environmental Engineering which included the flood raster shown here. The model output shows areas of 100 - year flooding in areas not previously analyzed for flooding. The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) is the authoritative source for developing Flood Insurance Rate Maps (FIRMs) and is largely referenced for other applications impacted by flooding. The departement of defense is not required to carry flood insurance and therefore, no FIRM maps are developed for Air Force Bases. The effort to map these areas is paid for by the Air Force to analyze risk for mission critical infrastructure.
