Greetings

Welcome to my portfolio page! I am a GIS professional with aspirations of becoming a Cartographer. With the help of University of Wisconsin Madison, I'm sure to succeed. For many years, I have created maps but as a GIS Analyst.

I'm lucky to work for both the Air Force and Space Force, where I manage and maintain enterprise spatial databases as part of the Air Force's GeoBase program. I get to make maps for Buckley Space Force Base - 460th Civil Engineering Squadron, which includes GIS support for Clear Space Force Station (SFS) in Alaska, Cavalier SFS in North Dakota, and Cape Cod SFS in Massachusetts. The spatial database I work on was designed for various data themes in support of engineering applications and programs. Each theme is used to create custom map products. Topics include architecture, facilities management, site plan development, emergency response plans, real property inventory, aviation, surveying, future planning, asset management, transportation, military, environmental planning, utility design, and construction. In addition to database management and cartography requirements, I provide planning and project consulting, including updating and implementing plans, programs, and forecasting requirements.

I have worked for the Department of Defense for the last fifteen years. I've had a lot of fun working on so many interesting maps and my portfolio is extensive but unfortunately this page includes very few examples due to information security concerns. However, I will do my best to select some examples of my work that showcase the variety of maps, I've made over the years.
